Agri-green, is a machine that produces water from Humidity

About Agri Green Agri-Green Foundation was conceptualized by Dr. Adnan Al Ramzani Al Naimi as a long-term goal for Agri-Green, as a company started in 2011. After the filing process for Agri-Green invention which had received more than a hundred and seventy (170) Gold Medals, Gold cups, Trophies, different type of certificates and other gold awards, Dr. Adnan, the Inventor persevered to organize and lead Agri-Green Foundation and conducted numerous training and seminars for young inventors, all over the State of Qatar, some of the Neighboring Countries and Germany, a very valuable support to educational institutions, local and international.
